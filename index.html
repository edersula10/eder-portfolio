03 // ABOUT

Your business. Online, done right.

I grew up in the Greater Toronto Area, surrounded by small business owners — tradespeople, restaurant owners, shop keepers, stylists. I saw how many of them had incredible businesses with decades of reputation behind them, but websites that hadn't been updated in years.

I build modern, fast, mobile-friendly websites that make your business look as professional online as it is in real life. No confusing tech talk, no hidden fees, no disappearing after launch.

I work with contractors, salons, barbershops, restaurants, retail shops, and any small business that wants a real digital presence. Every project is treated like it's my own — because my reputation is built on yours.
